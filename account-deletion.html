Account control

Account Deletion

Permanently request deletion of your Plito Rewards account and associated data.

Delete inside the app

  1. Open Plito Rewards.
  2. Sign in.
  3. Open Profile.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Review the warning and confirm.

Request by email

Email plitopay@gmail.com from the email linked to your account with the subject Plito Rewards Account Deletion Request.

Data removed

Account profile, authentication link, personal identifiers, points balance, reward history where applicable, notification tokens, preferences and unfulfilled voucher requests.

Limited retention

Minimal fraud-prevention, security, dispute or legal records may be retained where reasonably necessary and permitted by law.

Processing time

Requests are normally completed within 30 days after account-ownership verification.

Important

Deletion is permanent. Unused points and pending voucher requests may not be recoverable.
