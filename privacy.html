Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, Firebase user ID, Google account details when Google Sign-In is used, device identifiers, notification token, referral information, points balance, reward activity, redemption requests, support messages and app-usage data.

2. Information we do not collect

Plito Rewards does not request or collect UPI IDs, Paytm numbers, bank account numbers, IFSC codes or other cash-payout details for voucher redemption.

3. Why we use data

We use information to create and secure accounts, track points, review voucher redemption requests, deliver notifications, prevent fraud, provide support, improve app reliability and meet legal or policy obligations.

4. Device and security data

We may use device identifiers and security signals to enforce one-account rules, detect abuse and protect legitimate users.

5. Advertising and third-party services

The app may use Firebase, Google Sign-In, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Google AdMob and approved service providers. These providers may process data under their own policies.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data may be shared with service providers only when necessary for authentication, hosting, advertising, notifications, fraud prevention, support, legal compliance or voucher fulfilment.

7. Data retention

Account and reward records are retained while your account is active. After deletion, personal data is deleted or anonymised within a reasonable period, normally within 30 days, except limited records needed for fraud prevention, security, disputes or legal compliance.

8. Account deletion

You can delete your account inside the app through Profile → Delete Account, or follow the instructions on the Account Deletion page.

9. Children

Plito Rewards is intended for users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

10. Security

We use Firebase and reasonable technical and organisational safeguards. No online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes may be communicated through the app, website or notification.
